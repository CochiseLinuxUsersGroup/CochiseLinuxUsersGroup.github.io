Meeting Locations

Cochise Linux User Group meeting

Location: Sierra Vista Public Library, conference room

Address: 2600 E Tacoma St, Sierra Vista, AZ 85635,

Time & Date: 1:00pm, Fourth Saturday of the Month.

Description: This is our regular monthly meeting. We generally have a presentation on a variety of topics followed by a group discussion. This is followed by questions/problems by members which we attempt to answer/solve that day. After we finish at the library we normally normally go to a random restaurant for dinner and additional discussion.

Ubuntu Hour

Location: Schlotzsky's Cafe, back room

Address: 3900 E. Fry Blvd., Sierra Vista, AZ 85635

Time & Date: 6:00pm, Second Thursday of the Month.

Description: This is our unofficial second meeting of the month. It is primarily a social event where you can meet other Linux users in a very casual atmosphere where we discuss Ubuntu, other Linux distributions, and Free and Open Source software. It is a good time to ask any questions you may have regarding Linux or Free and Open Source Software.
