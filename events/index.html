Meeting Locations

Cochise Linux User Group meeting

Location: Warrior Healing Center, main conference room
Address: 1838 Paseo San Luis, Sierra Vista, AZ 85635
Time & Date: 5:00pm, Fourth Tuesday of the Month.

Description: This is our regular monthly meeting, where we start with a presentation on a variety of topics, followed by a group discussion. After that, we address any questions or issues members have, working together to find solutions on the spot. Once the meeting wraps up, we usually head to a local restaurant for dinner and more casual discussion.


New Update: We’ve also introduced a quarterly Workshop during this meeting. Members and new visitors can bring their computers, USB drives, or any problems they’ve been facing, and we’ll help resolve them right there.

Ubuntu Hour

Location: Schlotzsky's Cafe, back room
Address: 3900 E. Fry Blvd., Sierra Vista, AZ 85635
Time & Date: 6:00pm, Second Thursday of the Month.

Description: This is our informal second meeting of the month. It's mainly a social gathering where you can connect with other Linux users in a relaxed setting. We chat about Ubuntu, other Linux distributions, and Free and Open Source Software. It's a great opportunity to ask any questions you have about Linux or FOSS.

Cochise Linux User Group Dinner Meeting

Location: Resturant varies.
Time & Date: 5:00pm, Forth Tuesday of the Month.

Description: This is our quarterly dinner gathering, offering everyone a break from tech discussions and troubleshooting. We simply get together with family, friends, and neighbors—whoever wants to join—and enjoy a meal together.


Meeting times and dates are subject to change. To stay updated, please sign up for our mailing list, join our Discord, or follow our Facebook Group. You can also check our calendar for the latest meeting details.
