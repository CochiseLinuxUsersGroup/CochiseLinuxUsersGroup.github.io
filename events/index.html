Meeting Locations

Cochise Linux User Group meeting

Location: Warrior Healing Center, main conference room
Address: 1838 Paseo San Luis, Sierra Vista, AZ 85635
Time & Date: 5:00pm, Fourth Tuesday of the Month.

Description: This is our regular monthly meeting. We have a presentation on a variety of topics followed by a group discussion. This is followed by questions and or problems by members which we attempt to answer/solve that day. After we finish we normally go to a random restaurant for dinner and additional discussion.


A New Change in Meetings now we Split This Meeting into a Workshop once every quarter as well. Which members or new visitors can bring thier computers, usb flash drives or troubles they've been dealing with and have them solved at this meeting.

Ubuntu Hour

Location: Schlotzsky's Cafe, back room

Address: 3900 E. Fry Blvd., Sierra Vista, AZ 85635
Time & Date: 6:00pm, Second Thursday of the Month.

Description: This is our unofficial second meeting of the month. It is primarily a social event where you can meet other Linux users in a very casual atmosphere where we discuss Ubuntu, other Linux distributions, and Free and Open Source software. It is a good time to ask any questions you may have regarding Linux or Free and Open Source Software.
