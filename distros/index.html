CLUG Distro Supported List



These are the distrobutions you can expect to be willingly installed and supported at any Mini-workshop, CLUG or Ubuntu Hour, or our Workshop meetings.



## Supported Linux Distributions At our mini-workshops, CLUG meetings, Ubuntu Hours, and other workshop events, we **officially support and can assist with installing and troubleshooting** the following distributions: ### Fully Supported by Us - **Ubuntu** (all flavors) - **Linux Mint** (all flavors) - **LMDE** (Linux Mint Debian Edition) We are happy to offer **limited guidance** for installing other Linux distributions **if you bring your own installation media**. > **Note:** > Support for any distributions not listed above is **not guaranteed** and should not be expected. This support list is based on what most of our members actively use and are best able to troubleshoot.
