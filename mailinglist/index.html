Mailing List Instructions

Staying up to date with meeting dates or ask the group a question between meetings by signing up for the mailing list. This is done with a few easy steps.

1. Send an email to cochiselinux-request@freelists.org with subscribe in the subject field.
** You will receive an email to confirm your subscription.
2. Reply to that email leaving the message body intact.
** You will then be subscribed to the mailing list.


To send an email to the mailing list use cochiselinux@freelists.org and it will be sent to every person on the mailing list. Use the mailing list to ask questions of the members between meetings, to solve a problem that you are having with your computer or software, to post information that you believe might be of interest to the membership, to announce upcoming events, or to post minutes of activities that have occurred.
